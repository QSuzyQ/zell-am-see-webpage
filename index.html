Mountain, lake & glacier - these are the extensive holiday themes of the region. The indisputable centre of your holiday, whether ski holiday in winter or family holiday in summer, is the town of Zell am See.
The town of Zell am See is located at the base of the mountain Schmittenh√∂he and at the shores of the Lake Zell in close proximity to the glacier Kitzsteinhorn. On a peninsula, formed by the Schmittenbach stream, the town protrudes into Lake Zell and clearly states: "Here I am and you must come here if you want to enjoy your holiday in the Zell am See-Kaprun region."

Winter in Zell am See-Kaprun

Your breath comes out as clouds of steam and the sunshine hits the mountain slopes and ski pistes at an angle, signalling wonderful conditions for skiing and snowboarding. Providing top conditions for freeriding, winter & snowshoe hiking, cross-country skiing, tobogganing or extensive ski tours, fresh powder snow is waiting away from the ski pistes.


Summer in Zell am See-Kaprun

The days are long - the best time to spend a summer holiday between mountain, lake and glacier. Sporty activities are especially good early in the morning. as well as the admission to one of the lidos around Lake Zell is included.
